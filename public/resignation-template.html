Resignation Letter

15 June 2026

Board of Directors — Ariolas BV

Rue de la Loi 16, 1000 Brussels

Dear Board Members,

Please accept this letter as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as Chief Executive Officer at Ariolas BV.

After careful consideration, I have decided to step down from my role as CEO to pursue new opportunities and personal ventures. This was not an easy decision, and I am deeply grateful for the trust placed in me throughout my tenure.

In accordance with my contractual notice period, my last working day will be 31 August 2026. I am committed to ensuring a smooth transition and am happy to assist with handover procedures during this period.

I wish the company continued success.

Yours sincerely,

Joe Lernout

Chief Executive Officer
