Privacy Policy

Effective: May 31, 2026

Eburon AI ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our voice intelligence platform, mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an account — name, email address, profile photo, and authentication credentials via Google Sign-In or email/password.
  • Use voice features — audio recordings, transcriptions, and voice input data processed to provide real-time voice intelligence.
  • Connect third-party services — Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Tasks, YouTube) and WhatsApp data when you grant explicit authorization via OAuth.
  • Contact support — name, email, phone number, and message content.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — interactions with our Services, features used, session duration, and page views.
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — to authenticate sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

When you connect your Google Workspace or WhatsApp accounts, we access only the data necessary to fulfill your requests (e.g., reading calendar events to schedule meetings, sending WhatsApp messages on your behalf). We never access more data than what you explicitly authorize via OAuth scopes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the Services — process voice commands, generate AI responses, manage your account.
  • Improve and personalize — train and refine our AI models, personalize your experience, and develop new features.
  • Communication — respond to your inquiries, send service updates, and provide customer support.
  • Security and compliance — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access; comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent — when you explicitly authorize a specific data-sharing purpose.
  • Service providers — trusted third parties who help us operate the Services (e.g., cloud infrastructure, AI model hosting, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Voice recordings and transcriptions are retained for the duration of your account activity unless you request deletion. You may delete your account and associated data at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, regular security audits, and secure authentication flows. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data and account.
  • Portability — request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Opt-out — decline certain data collection practices (e.g., marketing communications).
  • Withdraw consent — revoke Google Workspace or WhatsApp access at any time via your account settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@eburon.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Services integrate with third-party platforms you explicitly connect:

  • Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Tasks, YouTube) — accessed only via OAuth 2.0 with your explicit consent. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.
  • WhatsApp — messages are processed through our backend to facilitate voice-controlled messaging.
  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and storage services.
  • Gemini API (Google) — AI voice processing and natural language understanding.
  • Supabase — additional data storage and real-time features.

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing data handling. We encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers and service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required by applicable law.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or through a prominent notice on our Services. Your continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@eburon.ai
  • Website: https://eburon.ai
